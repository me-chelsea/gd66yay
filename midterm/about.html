About/Contact

GEECCS is founded by Brandon J. Reeder a graduate of the Bachelor of Science in Wildlife Ecology and Conservation program through the University of Nevada, Reno. Brandon's life work is focused on fostering relationships with local and government agencies and private land owners to work towards a restored and thriving habitat of native flora and fauna throughout the region and nationwide. He has worked for the past five years to restore habitats for and populations of the Lahotan Cutthroat Trout, working with Trout Unlimited.

We are happy to serve you to the best of our scope and abilities. Please contact us with a call/text or email for any and all inquiries and comments. Thank you for visiting and we look forward to working with you.
